FAQ'S

What types of donations (or) contributions do you accept?

Donations can be made via credit card, check, wire transfer, and cash.

How do I donate?

You can easily donate your contribution on the website or through the mail. Click on the ‘donate’ button (in the header) and all charitable options will be made available.

What address do I send my contribution?

You can mail your donation check directly to Flood Rescue Foundation, 11601 Blackhawk Drive, Austin, TX 78759-4208. A recipe for your donation will be returned to you by mail within 30days.

How do I make a wire transfer donation?

Contact the Foundation and we will provide you with the Routing and Account Number for your wire transfer.

Is my donation tax deductible?

You may be entitled to a tax deduction for your contribution, subject to general limitations depending upon circumstances, including whether or not you itemize deductions. Please consult your tax advisor to determine if you are entitled to a tax deduction.

How do I make non-cash donations?

We are not set up to receive non-cash donations. Try your local Goodwill, Salvation Army, or Veterans’ support organizations.

How do I donate an estate to the Foundation?

You can name the Flood Rescue Foundation as a beneficiary in your will/estate for a specific amount or a percentage, or designate the foundation as a beneficiary of your life insurance policy. We suggest consulting a legal professional before drafting a will or trust.

How can I print my donation receipt?

Your donation receipt is emailed to immediately. Print out the email and submit to your tax accountant along with your tax documents.

How do I modify my recurring donation?

Login to your account and update your recurring donation amount directly.
